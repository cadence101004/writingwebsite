Cadence Fong's Work

Traffic isn’t random, it just looks like it

Welcome!

This site is a collection of my academic work, research, and writing as a computer science student at CU Boulder. I wanted to take my original assignments and turn them into something that feels more real and readable.

My Research Topic

My research focuses on traffic modeling and how we can use math and computation to understand traffic flow. At first, traffic just feels like something random and annoying, but it actually follows patterns that can be studied, predicted, and even improved.

How the Studies Connect

The sources I looked at all approach traffic differently, using simulations, probability, and real-world data. Even though they use different methods, they all try to answer the same question: how can we better understand and control traffic systems? Together, they show that traffic is not chaotic, but something that can be analyzed and optimized.

Why This Matters

I created this site to make my research more accessible and less rigid than a traditional paper. Revising my work for a broader audience made me think more about clarity and how to explain technical ideas without making them confusing or boring.
