About Me

the person behind everything on this site

My name is Cadence Fong, and I am currently an undergraduate computer science student at the University of Colorado, Boulder. I’m especially interested in web design and software development because I love creating projects that blend creativity with functionality. Topics such as video editing, video games, and user experience greatly inspire me, as they allow me to explore the more visual and human–interactive side of technology. Whether I'm coding a responsive website or designing a clean interface, I enjoy finding ways to bring ideas to life through thoughtful, hands–on creation.

I chose traffic modeling as my research topic because it connects math, computer science, and real-world problems in a way that actually feels meaningful. Traffic is something everyone experiences, but most people don’t think about how complex it really is. Through my research, I explored how different models try to represent traffic patterns and how those models can be used to improve efficiency and better understand real-world systems.
